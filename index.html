About Me

Hello!
I'm Gerardo Michele Mussuto, a former dancer who found his new passion in the world of technology.
After years of experience on stage, I decided to embark on a new educational journey at the DCI school to become a full-stack web developer.
My passion for theater and opera is reflected in my aspiration to develop websites for theaters around the world and for emerging artists.
I dream of combining my stage knowledge with my technical skills to create engaging and immersive digital experiences.
Outside of work, I enjoy spending my free time playing video games, taking walks in nature, and exploring new travel destinations.
I'm always looking for new challenges and creative opportunities to grow both professionally and personally.
I'm excited to share my passion for technology and the entertainment world through my work and to create experiences that inspire and engage people worldwide.

Weather App

Trivia Game

Welcome to the World Trivia Game, where you can test your knowledge of different countries around the globe. This fun and interactive trivia game challenges you with at least 10 questions per country and includes timer, sound effects, and confetti for correct answers!

PokémonAPI

A simple web application that allows users to search for a Pokémon by name and display its image (sprite) using the PokéAPI. Users can enter a Pokémon's name and see the front-facing sprite if it is available.

Opera Project

This JavaScript code was created with a passion for theater and opera. Its purpose is to provide people with a clear and simple way to discover the available shows in Berlin during a specific time of the year. In this case, September was chosen, a month rich with operatic offerings in the German capital.

Code Square Chat

Code Square Chat (CSC) is a real-time chat application built for seamless and secure communication. It includes user registration, public chat rooms, private messaging, and a responsive UI optimized for mobile and desktop devices. CSC is the final project of the DCI Full Stack Web Development course.
